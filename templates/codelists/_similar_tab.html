About Similarity Computation

Similarity is computed using the Jaccard index, which measures the proportion of codes shared between two codelists:

Jaccard Similarity = (Shared Codes) / (Total Unique Codes)

For example, if two codelists share 20 codes and have 100 unique codes in total, their similarity would be 20%.
